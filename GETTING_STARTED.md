## ğŸ¤ How to Use

### Basic Usage
```
1. Click in any text field (Notes, Email, Messages, etc.)
2. Press Alt+Space
3. Speak clearly: "This is a test message"
4. Press Esc
5. Your text appears!
```

### Two Modes

**Standard Mode** (Alt+Space)
- Fast and accurate
- Direct transcription
- Best for most uses

**AI Rewrite Mode** (Alt+Shift+Space)
- Enhanced with AI
- Grammar corrections
- Better for formal writing
